Black Text on White Background - Safari/Chrome Extensions

Pretty straightforward functionality here...activating the plugin will attempt to take the page you're viewing & update the css of elements which are white text on top of black/dark backgrounds, and turn them into black text on a white background.

There are a couple of other style changes that take place - removing some background images & updating link styles - but it's all pretty basic.

Safari extension (btowb.safariextz)

Chrome extension (btowb-chrome.crx)
